Why this exists

Built out of frustration with YouTube.

I watch a lot of YouTube. So do you. I also travel a lot and download videos for flights. It is often difficult to download, with videos being hidden behind paywalls or ads. This should work without the additional hassle.

You can also skim, search and review the transcripts, because often you are trying to find a quote, or feed content into an AI.

Features
📥

Download Videos

  • Any public YouTube video
  • Choose quality up to 1080p
  • MP4 format, ready to play anywhere
  • Saves directly to your device
  • Works offline after download
📝

Convert Videos to Text

  • Auto-transcripts, no API key needed
  • Plain text or with timestamps
  • 30s / 60s chunks ready for RAG pipelines
  • Export as .txt, .csv, or .json
  • Click any line to jump to that moment
